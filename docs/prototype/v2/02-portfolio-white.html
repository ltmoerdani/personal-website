(About)

Laksmana "Oci" Tri Moerdani. Twenty years in, I keep seeing the same shape: a company ships a platform, and six months later nobody in the room can say what it was meant to fix. The standup still runs. The roadmap still grows. The original question is gone.

I co-founded Reka Cipta Digital because most product owners have never been forced to write down, in one sentence a CFO would fund, what the thing is actually for. So that's the first thing I make people do. It is uncomfortable. I've been told it feels like homework. Currently doing this inside Pro Archery, 12,000+ SKUs, retail and consumer platform launching September.

The other thing I do is build. MyArchery.id runs scoring for most archery events in Indonesia, built because three friends were tired of running national qualifiers on spreadsheets. Blazz is WhatsApp-native SaaS for warung owners. OpenCode puts 30+ AI models inside GitHub Copilot, and I shipped it because I wanted them there. None of these started as product strategy. They started as irritation.

How I work with you.

You get me, directly. Not an account manager, not a junior routed through a senior. When a project needs design or engineering weight, Reka Cipta Digital, the studio I co-founded, supplies it. I stay your single point of contact. That's the whole model.

Product Strategy & Discovery

A three-week sprint. Week one, we write down what the problem actually is, in one sentence. Week two, we build the smallest thing that could prove us wrong. Week three, we find out.
